Professionalism Matters

Keynote on Alternatives for SlideShow

In my second year, I did CMNS 251 (Professional Report Writing) which focuses on research and critical evaluation of information. It is important to work professionally in all our courses. We worked on assignment to deliver a presentation to show alternatives to Slide Show presentations.

Each member worked on their part, however, it was unethical the way the work was divided. I created the presentation that includes videos and info graphics. The presentation included proper citation and a live video link so students could stream the presentation from their home as well!

All the work delivered was easy to catch. The artifact attached shows the keynote on Alternatives for SlideShow.
